Goals

I come from Darkness Will Arise. We are going to design Verse, which is a virtual shopping world for users so that
1. They can shop online.
2. They can communicate with friends during the experience of online shopping.
With these visions, we hope Verse can provide the target audience an alternative to shop and interact with others.

learning and execution process

Empathize

Brainstorm

We develop our idea with a mind map.

In order to understand "social with people", we start thinking of how a virtual shopping experience should look like based on researching the reason for physical shopping
We found that customer shop physically because of the following reason:
1. They need expert guidance to make the right purchasing decisions.
2. Physical shopping increases visibility
3. Physical shopping offers a sense of security to the clients and they trust more
4. They need ‘Personal attention’ from the staff
5. They can ‘try out’ the products before buying
6. Impulse spending is more apparent in physical stores, compared to online shopping where customers browse more.

Interpret

POV

In order to understand our target users and their need, We constructe a Point-Of-View based on the customer's view: For those normal and for those have special need.

Ideate

After we identified the insight the users may face when going to buy goods, we have set certain sub-goals:
1. They can look for their products in the VR world
2. They can seek help from the staffs.
3. They can interact with other in the process of shopping.
4. They can understand the meaning of the sentence regradless of the language or the ability to talk or not.
